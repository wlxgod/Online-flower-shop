How Do We Make It?

Communication and Trust

Our collaboration has not always been smooth sailing.
Early in the project, we found that if we didn't give everyone a clear task and deadline, our teammates would be lazy until reaching the deadline. So we began to set strict weekly tasks and believed that the quality of each other's achievement is satisfying. In this way, our division of labor becomes very intuitive and easy, we can complete a lot of tasks in a short period, while everyone is very chilled but not tired.
